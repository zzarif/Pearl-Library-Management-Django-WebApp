Read About Us

Pearl Library is a fictional library run by a charity organisation. They aim to spread beneficial knowledge among poor people by giving them free access to books which are expensive to buy individually. All types of books are present in the library including Islamic books, textbooks, math books, geography books and so on.

Our mission is to excel in supporting the academic and scholarly endeavor of our users, in their core instructional and research requirements through utilizing the best possible systems and services.
